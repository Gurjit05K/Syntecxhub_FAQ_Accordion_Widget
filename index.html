Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services and products.

What is your return policy?

We offer a 30-day return policy for all unused products in their original packaging. Returns must be initiated within 30 days of purchase, and the items must be in resellable condition. Refunds are processed within 5-7 business days after we receive the returned items.

How long does shipping take?

Standard shipping takes 5-7 business days. Express shipping is available for an additional fee and delivers within 2-3 business days. International shipping times vary by location but typically take 10-14 business days. You'll receive tracking information as soon as your order ships.

Do you offer technical support?

Yes, we provide free technical support for all our products. Our support team is available Monday through Friday from 9 AM to 6 PM EST. You can contact us via email at support@example.com or call our toll-free number at 1-800-123-4567. We also have an extensive knowledge base and video tutorials on our website.

Can I upgrade my plan later?

Absolutely! You can upgrade your plan at any time from your account dashboard. When you upgrade, you'll be charged the prorated difference for the remainder of your billing cycle. Downgrades are also possible but will take effect at the start of your next billing cycle.

Is my payment information secure?

Yes, we take security very seriously. We use industry-standard SSL encryption to protect your data during transmission. We are PCI-DSS compliant and never store your full credit card information on our servers. All payments are processed through secure, trusted payment gateways.

Do you offer discounts for nonprofits?

Yes, we offer a 20% discount for registered nonprofit organizations. To qualify, please contact our sales team with proof of your nonprofit status. Educational institutions also qualify for special pricing. Discounts cannot be combined with other promotional offers.
